# Stop Killing Games Community Jam

This is the first SKG Community Jam, conducted in July of 2025, the hot month of the final
make or break of SKG movement, when we tried to do everything to push that petition
progress bar forward, and some of us made games for the jam!

Good times!

In the end the petition succeeded, we made 233 games, Ross played the winning entries
and everyone had fun for a good cause :)

[Check out the wonderful games created in the jam](../skgjam2025-entries/).

The jam took 10 days, and 7 days for community judging.
