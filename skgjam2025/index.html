Stop Killing Games Community Jam

This was the first SKG Community Jam, conducted in July of 2025. The hot month of the final make or break of SKG movement, when we tried to do everything to push that petition progress bar forward.

Good times!

The jam was organized with very little advance. Fortunately, a lot of people helped spread the news of the jam! Ross Scott made a youtube post about it, letting people know of our little "crunch session" :D Itch.io featured the jam on their page. The good people from the SKG community discord contacted their reddit friends at r/StopKillingGames, who created a nice post about it.

The theme for the jam was "Limited Lifetime".

In the end the petition succeeded, the jam had over 1000 joiners who made 233 games. Everyone had good fun for a good cause :)

Big thanks to Ross, who helped to promote the jam and also played 20 top placed entries on his stream!
